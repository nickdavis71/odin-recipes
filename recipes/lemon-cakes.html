Lemon Cakes

Description

Mini Lemon Cakes (Makes 9 Individual Cakes)

Ingredients

  • ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 8½ ounces all-purpose flour (about 2 cups), plus more for dusting
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Butter a 9x13-inch baking pan and dust it with flour, tapping out any excess.
  2. Line the bottom of the pan with parchment paper rounds cut to fit.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, cream together the butter and sugar on medium-high speed.
  5. With the mixer on medium-low speed, beat in 1 egg at a time.
  6. Increase the speed to medium and beat for 1 minute more, or until light and ribbony.
  7. Add the dry ingredients and mix on low speed until combined.
  8. Add the buttermilk, lemon zest, and lemon juice and beat on medium-high speed until the batter is light and fluffy.
  9. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 25 to 30 minutes, until a tester inserted into the cake comes out clean.
  10. Remove from the oven and let cool in the pan for 30 minutes.
  11. Invert the cake onto a wire rack, remove the parchment paper, and let cool for 30 more minutes.
