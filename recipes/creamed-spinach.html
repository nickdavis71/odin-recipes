Creamed Spinach

Description

Homemade Creamed Spinach (Makes Approximately 1 Cup)

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup spinach, rinsed and dried
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. In a large saute pan, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat until shimmering.
  2. Add the spinach and cook, stirring, until bright green and wilted, about 2 minutes.
  3. Remove the pan from the heat.
  4. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium-high heat.
  5. Once the butter begins to bubble, add the flour and whisk to combine.
  6. Cook, stirring, until the raw flour smell dissipated, 30 to 60 seconds.
  7. While whisking continuously, add the milk in a steady stream and whisk until completely incorporated.
  8. Cook over medium-low heat until the bechamel is thickened, about 3 minutes.
  9. Add the bechamel to the spinach and simmer for about 1 minute, until the flavors are incorporated.
  10. Season with salt and pepper.
